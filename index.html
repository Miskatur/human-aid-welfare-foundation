Last month, a devastating flash flood struck the northeastern districts of Sylhet and Sunamganj in Bangladesh.
The continuous heavy rainfall in the north-eastern Indian region terribly affected more than 2 million people who live

Fenchuganj, and many more upazillas of Sylhet as well as several upazilas of Sunamganj and Netrokona districts have been inundated. They need our help to overcome this situation. So be a part of us.
